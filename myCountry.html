Where is Indonesia?

Southeast Asia

Indonesia stands as the largest archipelago globally, its vast expanse spread across the Indian and Pacific Oceans. Positioned along the volatile Pacific Ring of Fire, it is both blessed and burdened by its geology, prone to natural disasters like earthquakes and volcanic eruptions. Surrounded by its maritime borders with Malaysia, Papua New Guinea, and East Timor, Indonesia's diverse landscapes and cultures thrive amidst the dynamic forces of nature.
